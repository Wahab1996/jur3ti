Infant Supplementation Recommendations

Supplement Arabic Indication & Dose Duration
Vitamin D فيتامين د All infants (breastfed or formula-fed):
400 IU (10 µg) daily, starting within first few days of life. 		Continue until intake ≥1L fortified formula/day or after 12 months.
Iron الحديد Term breastfed infants: 1 mg/kg/day from 4–6 months until iron-rich foods introduced.
Preterm (<37 weeks): 2–3 mg/kg/day from 2 weeks until 12 months. 		Adjust per dietary intake and hemoglobin screening.

Usually 1 mL/day infant multivitamin preparation. 		As clinically indicated.

Practical Notes for Family Medicine Practice

  • Postpone vaccination in case of moderate/severe acute illness with fever.
  • Mild URTI or low-grade fever is not a contraindication.
  • Premature infants: vaccinate according to chronological age (not corrected age).
  • BCG: defer in symptomatic HIV or immunodeficiency until specialist review.
  • Influenza vaccine: yearly from 6 months; 2 doses the first year (≥4 weeks apart).
  • Document all vaccines clearly on the child’s MOH immunization card.
