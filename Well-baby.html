Family Medicine Resident - Well Baby Clinic Guide

Resident Responsibilities

  • Perform thorough physical examination of newborns and infants.
  • Measure and plot growth parameters: weight, length, and head circumference.
  • Screen developmental milestones appropriate for age.
  • Administer vaccinations according to the national immunization schedule.
  • Counsel parents on nutrition, breastfeeding, and infant care best practices.
  • Identify signs of common pediatric illnesses and refer if needed.
  • [translate:تقديم الدعم والنصيحة للأهالي بشأن نمو الطفل]
  • [translate:مراقبة وتقييم التطور الحركي واللغوي للرضيع]
  • Maintain accurate documentation for each visit.

Equipment and Resources Needed

  • Infant scale, length measuring board, and head circumference tape.
  • Stethoscope and blood pressure cuff suitable for infants.
  • Up-to-date vaccine supply and immunization guidelines.
  • Educational leaflets and materials for parents in English and Arabic [translate:مواد تعليمية للأهالي].
  • Access to electronic health records for documentation.
  • Emergency pediatric care equipment [translate:معدات طوارئ للأطفال].
  • Private and child-friendly examination space.

Immunization Schedule

Age Vaccine Notes
At birth BCG, Hepatitis B [translate:اللقاحات الأولية]
2 months DTP, Polio, Hib, Hepatitis B, PCV First dose of pentavalent and pneumococcal vaccines.
4 months DTP, Polio, Hib, Hepatitis B, PCV (2nd doses) Follow-up doses.
6 months Influenza (seasonal), Hepatitis B (3rd dose) Annual flu vaccine recommended.
12-15 months MMR, Varicella, Booster doses [translate:اللقاحات المعززة]

Developmental Milestones to Assess

  • Motor: lifting head, rolling over, sitting, crawling, walking.
  • Language: cooing, babbling, first words.
  • Social: smiling, waving, stranger anxiety.
  • [translate:متابعة تطور المهارات الحركية واللغوية والاجتماعية]

Parent Counseling Points

  • Breastfeeding techniques and benefits.
  • Introduction of age-appropriate complementary foods.
  • Safe sleeping practices to reduce risk of SIDS.
  • Maintaining hygiene and preventing common infections.
  • When to seek urgent medical care.
  • [translate:نصائح حول التغذية والنوم الآمن ورعاية الطفل]
  • [translate:متى يجب استشارة الطبيب فوراً]
