Family Medicine Resident - Well Baby Clinic

What the Resident Should Do

  • Conduct thorough newborn and infant physical examinations.
  • Monitor growth parameters: weight, length, head circumference.
  • Administer and recommend age-appropriate vaccines per immunization schedule.
  • Provide anticipatory guidance on nutrition, sleep, and development.
  • Screen for common pediatric illnesses and developmental delays.
  • Counsel parents on breastfeeding and safe infant care practices.
  • [translate:تقديم الدعم والنصيحة للأهل حول نمو الطفل]
  • [translate:مراقبة التطورات الحركية واللغوية للرضيع]
  • Document all findings and updates in the medical record accurately.

Essential Needs for the Resident

  • Access to up-to-date immunization guidelines and growth charts.
  • Basic examination tools: stethoscope, infant scale, measuring tape.
  • Educational materials for parents in both English and Arabic [translate:مواد تعليمية للأهل].
  • Support from pediatricians and senior family medicine doctors for consultation.
  • Documentation and electronic health record system access.
  • Private and safe examination room suitable for infants.
  • [translate:معدات طوارئ للأطفال] for handling urgent cases.
  • Time allocated for parental counseling and follow-up planning.
